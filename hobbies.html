places i want to visit

Travel sharpens perspective in a way that no course does. These are the cities i want to get to:

🏵 Tokyo, Japan

the intersection of technology and culture. every engineer i respect has a japan story. also: the food, the transit system, and the density of innovation in one city is unmatched.

🏭 San Francisco, USA

silicon valley. yes, it is expensive and chaotic. but i want to be in the room where the builders are. the concentration of people working on hard problems is a magnet for me.

🏴 Zurich, Switzerland

ETH Zurich produces some of the best CS and AI research in the world. i want to visit both for the academic atmosphere and because the alps are right there.

🚩 Dubai, UAE

a city that built itself in one generation. the ambition of the engineering is inspiring, and the StartUp community there makes it feel like a second home.
